
MOA :-
it pulls in water from the body to the large intestine.
which :
    - softens the stools
    - tolerates insufficient fluids intake with ispaghula

Interactions :-
Ketoconazole
Azithromycin
Aluminium Hydroxide
Oral neomycin
laxatives
antacids


Contraindications :-
Allergy to lactulose (may cause anaphylaxis)
Galactosemia (patients on galactose-restricted diet)
diabetes mellitus
people with lactose intolerance

Side effects :-
Diarrhoea
Flatulence
Abdominal Discomfort
Nausea
Vomiting
(Diarrhea and abdominal pain occur due to a high dose.)

Safety :-
safe with alcohol
safe with pregnancy
safe with breast-feeding
safe to drive while on this medicine
no renal impairment
in case of overdose: -> Taking more than the recommended dose of this medication may cause abdominal pain, electrolyte imbalance and diarrhea, which may last for a couple of days
